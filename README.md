<p>
  Nice to meet you, I am Renato Machado, and right now, my biggest motivation is using technology to create things that improve, even if just a little bit, the way people live and interact with the world. I was born in Brazil, but my dual citizenship allowed me to experience Canadian culture firsthand for a long period of my life. That unique experience not only massively improved my English but also gave me a rich perspective that I take with me and try to apply to everything I do.
</p>

<p> 
  I'm currently wrapping up my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science. Software development is at my core, but what really sparks my interest is cybersecurity, cloud computing, and data architecture. Whenever I take on a project, I like to dive in headfirst and give it my absolute best.
</p>

<p>
  Away from the keyboard, I am a guy who appreciates a good meal, the company of my loved ones and animals, and a quiet place to gather my thoughts. I am a huge fan of the Tolkien universe and, whenever I have some time to spare, I enjoy playing games (especially online). I'm always looking to learn something new from GitHub and the tech community, and to contribute in any way I can.
</p>
